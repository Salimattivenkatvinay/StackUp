Frequently asked questions

What happens if no one bids for the Chit amount ?

The program automatically picks a member out of the remaining members and transfers the fund to his account.

What if two people bid the same value during an auction?

The program doesn’t allow two users to bid the same amount, it throws a warning if any user is trying to bid an amount that is already bid.

Can I change/delete my bid?

Yes, you can modify or delete your existing bid until the deadline

How is a chit fund a better saving option?

By joining a chit fund, a person is forced to save a small part of his income, to meet his planned/unplanned investments or expenditure. In case of contingency, borrowing from the chit fund is much easier, simpler, faster and cheaper. In case a person wants to save till the last, the return a subscriber gets by way of chit dividends is much higher.
